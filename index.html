Our Story

Crafted for Those
Who Seek the Finest

Founded in 2008 by visionary hotelier Isabelle Fontaine, Maison Dorée was born from a singular vision — to create a sanctuary where every detail whispers luxury and every moment becomes a cherished memory.

From hand-selected artworks to bespoke amenities sourced from across the globe, our commitment to excellence is woven into the very fabric of this extraordinary place.
